About me

Front-end developer from Carletonville, South Africa

I was born and raised in Carletonville, I pursued my education at North-West University, specializing in Human Resources. However, my journey took a turn when I discovered a passion for coding, prompting me to delve into this field through self-learning. Seeking structured guidance, I applied to SheCodes Foundation, where I completed two workshops - the Beginner Workshop and the SheCodes Plus workshop - gaining foundational knowledge in coding and advanced skills in web development.
